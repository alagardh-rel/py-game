How to Play

  1. Enter your name and click Start Game
  2. You'll see a 30x30 grid of letters
  3. Find hidden English words in the grid
  4. Type words in the search box (minimum 3 letters)
  5. Score 1 point per letter found
  6. You have 60 seconds to find as many words as possible
  7. Submit your score to compete on the leaderboard
